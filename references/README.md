## Reading Guide

**Start here (foundations):**
- Refs 2 & 3: Complete reviews of NV physics and magnetometry — essential reading.
- Ref 4 (Barry 2020): Definitive guide to sensitivity, noise sources, and ODMR modes.

**Low-cost / practical implementations:**
- Ref 7 (Webb 2021): Compact diamond NV magnetometer — closest to this project's spirit.
- Ref 5 (Clevenson 2015): Light-trapping geometry for sensitivity enhancement.

**Hardware datasheets:**
- Refs 9 & 10: ADF4351 PLL and ESP32-C3 microcontroller.
